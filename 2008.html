The 3D digital instrument unit displays all the information required for driving within the driver’s field of vision, such as navigation and driving assistance systems. CONNECTED SERVICES AT YOUR FINGERTIPS

With PEUGEOT connected services, you can save time and optimise your budget. With the My PEUGEOT application you have a service giving you daily help and comfort.

Your journeys are optimised (traffic management, service station search, danger-zone alerts etc.). In case of a breakdown or an accident, we are there for you 24/7.

You can even control the charging and thermal pre-conditioning of your electric PEUGEOT remotely.

SPLIT-LEVEL BOOT

The boot space combines with the rear 2/3-1/3 split-fold bench seat to offer you a large transport volume, adjustable according to your needs. The double-level boot floor, in the high position, allows you to load and unload items quickly and easily. In the low position, the boot volume is enlarged.
